üöÄ What is OxiZ?

OxiZ is a pure Rust SMT (Satisfiability Modulo Theories) solver compiled to WebAssembly. It brings powerful constraint solving capabilities directly to your browser, with no server required!

  • Pure Rust - No C/C++ dependencies, fully safe and modern
  • Browser-native - Runs entirely in your browser, no backend needed
  • Fast & Efficient - Optimized WASM binary under 2MB
  • SMT-LIB2 Compatible - Standard SMT-LIB2 syntax support
