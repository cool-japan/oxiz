What is Craig Interpolation?

Given an UNSAT formula partitioned into A and B, Craig interpolation computes an interpolant I such that:

  • A implies I
  • I and B is UNSAT
  • I only contains symbols common to A and B

This is useful for modular verification, abstraction refinement, and invariant generation.
